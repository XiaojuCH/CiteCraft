# Consulting Playbook: Reviewable AI Outputs

Research and consulting teams adopt AI tooling faster when the first output already looks like something a manager can review and reuse.

## Practical guidance

Use one fixed sample project per wedge so teams can quickly understand the workflow without debating setup.

Prefer evidence-bound deliverables over broad agent autonomy, because reviewers care more about verification than orchestration.

Show the jump from a deliverable claim back to a source chunk, even when the locator is only a section, file path, or page label in P0.

## Risks

Strategy teams lose trust quickly if AI output sounds polished but cannot be traced to market evidence.

Perfect export fidelity can wait until the content and trace quality already survive stakeholder review.
